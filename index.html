Installation Guide (Quick Overview)

  1. Remove shackles from recovery points.
  2. Stretch each rattle ring over the shackle pin or base.
  3. Ensure even alignment on both sides.
  4. Reattach shackles and tighten securely.
  5. Drive and enjoy a rattle-free ride.

FAQ Section

1. What are rattle rings used for?
 They prevent metal-on-metal contact between shackles and mounts, reducing vibration and noise during driving or towing.

2. Will these fit my D-ring shackles?
 Yes, they’re designed for universal fit and work with most ¾-inch shackles used in offroad recovery setups.

3. Are they durable in extreme weather?
 Absolutely. The silicone material resists UV damage, moisture, and temperature fluctuations.
